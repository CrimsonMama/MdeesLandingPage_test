Our Products


The Fish


And elegant bronze and alloy metal sculpture depicting the life and demise of the tuna fish. Inspired by the sea fishermen off the coast of Puerto Vallarta, Mexico. Designed by one of our top sculpturists Finton Shaw. The sculpture is 7ft in height and 3ft width. It weighs in at 320lbs.

The Mermother and Child.


A very imaginative sculpture depicting the link between mother and child as well as the more inherent one between life and the sea. A deeply symbolic and meaningful piece to display in any elegant garden as a center attraction. This sculpture is 5ft 5 inches in height and 3ft width. It weighs 287lbs.

Great Eagle


Made from refined copper and bronze metals this great sculpture will clearly dominate any centerpiece attraction. A stunning and powerful symbol of strength, beauty and power. The sculpture is 6ft in height and 3ft 5 inches width. It weighs in at 315lbs.
